HOW TO PLAY DRUMS ONLINE?

Use your computer keyboard or click the drum kit to play the virtual drums. You can use the following shortcuts:

  • Crash cymbal : y
  • Ride cymbal : u
  • Hi-hat (closed) : r
  • Hi-hat (open) : e
  • Hi-hat (foot) : c
  • High tom : g
  • Low tom : h
  • Floor tom : j
  • Snare Drum : s
  • Snare drum (cross stick) : d
  • Bass drum : x

Drummers often use their right hand to play the cymbals (crash cymbal, ride cymbal, and hi-hat), and their left hand to play the snare drum. When using shortcuts, use the left hand to play both the snare drum and the bass drum.
