HOW TO PLAY PIANO ONLINE?

Use your computer keyboard or click the piano keys to play the piano.

  • Black keys : Use the number rows and first alphabet row (from q to t) to play the black keys of the piano or simply click the buttons to play them!
  • White keys : Use the second alphabet row (from a to ') and the third alphabet row (from z to /) to play the white keys of the piano or simply click the buttons to play!
